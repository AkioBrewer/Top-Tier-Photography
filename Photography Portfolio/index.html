About Us

At Top Tier Photography, we don’t conform to one style. We customize the style of photography towards your specific desires. Mike Moreland’s commercial advertising background equips him with the knowledge to photograph your creative ideas in any condition! Whether that be indoors or outdoors, with natural lighting or studio lighting, or in full sun or low light, our team will understand exactly what your goals are and provide you with experienced and informed shoot suggestions. Mike is also familiar with many beautiful and uncharted locations around North Georgia, so his work offers a broader variety over other local photographers.

Our Services

You want to set up a portrait shoot but not sure what you want? This is why our consultation is so important. We discuss what your goals for the shoot are, before we ever click the shutter. Will these photos hang in your home, or be compiled into a custom family heirloom album? Do you have empty walls you want to fill with beautiful art? Mike can come to your home and help discuss where the portraits will hand and the best size for your space. Maybe you want to create a family gallery that incorporates images from throughout the years combined with this year’s commissioned pieces. We make it happen! If your home has limited wall space, we love telling the story of your family through our custom designed albums. When you start with a well thought out plan, we take it a step further and provide you with the best locations, clothing and length of session to achieve your dreams.
